Process

Modules

Each company customises their recruitment process with the modules that make sense for them.

01

AI CV Screening

The company defines the ideal profile and AI automatically screens submitted CVs, identifying the candidates with the highest compatibility.

02

Psychometric & Technical Test

The candidate answers a personalised questionnaire with technical and behavioural questions, evaluating competencies and cultural fit objectively.

03

Interview with the Company

Only the best profiles advance to a face-to-face or online interview with the company, no time wasted on out-of-profile candidates.

Platform

Everything you need in one place

Personalized AI tests

Real-time dashboard
