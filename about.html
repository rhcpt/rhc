About RHC

Building the recruitment platform

A team using AI to help companies hire the right people.

01
The problem

Hiring is slow, expensive, and disconnected from what really matters.

Traditional recruitment hasn't changed in decades. Hiring teams drown in CVs, candidates get lost in inboxes, and the best people slip through the cracks because no one had time to read past the first 30 résumés.

02
What we build

An end-to-end platform that lets HR teams focus on people, not paperwork.

RHC combines AI-powered CV screening, personalised psychometric and technical assessment, and a clean, modern experience for both recruiters and candidates. Every part of the product is designed to remove friction and let people make better hiring decisions.

03
How we work

Close to the people who'll actually use the product.

We work directly with HR teams, founders and recruitment specialists to shape RHC around real workflows, not assumptions. We believe great hiring shouldn't be a privilege of large enterprises, it should be accessible, transparent, and powered by the best technology available today.
