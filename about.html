We're building the recruitment platform we always wished existed, one where AI choses candidates.

Who we are

RHC was born out of a simple frustration: traditional recruitment is slow, expensive, and often disconnected from what really matters. Hiring teams drown in CVs, candidates get lost in inboxes, and the best people slip through the cracks.

We're a Portuguese team building an end-to-end recruitment platform that combines AI-powered screening, psychometric assessment, and a clear, modern experience for both recruiters and candidates. Every part of the product is designed to remove friction and let people make better hiring decisions.

We work closely with HR teams, founders, and recruitment specialists to shape the product around real workflows. We believe great hiring shouldn't be a privilege of large enterprises, it should be accessible, transparent, and powered by the best technology available today.
